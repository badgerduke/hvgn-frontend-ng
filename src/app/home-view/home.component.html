Welcome to the HVGN genealogy website

where HVGN stands for: Hamacher Vosen Gempeler and Nipple
1936 - Christian Gempeler, Florence Imogene Nipple, Theresa Vosen, Peter Hamacher


These are the four surnames of my (John Eric Hamacher) four paternal great-grandparents. This website is of potential value to anyone researching their family history in south-central Wisconsin, especially Dane County. Click here to view the family tree.

If you see a mistake, want to comment or have information I might find interesting, e-mail Eric at erichamacher@protonmail.com. Over time, I will have additional people, information and sources.

Very special thanks go out to the following people for their time and information: Lynn Betlock, Leo Hamacher, Millie Hamacher, Penny Nipple, and many more.
